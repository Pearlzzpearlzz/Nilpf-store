NILPF PROGRAM CHARTER AND PRINCIPLES

Purpose
The NILPF framework establishes a structured environment that promotes independence, dignity, and personal responsibility within shared living environments.

Program Philosophy
The NILPF program operates under the principle that individuals are capable of maintaining independent lives when provided with a respectful and structured housing environment.

Foundational Principles

1. Independence
Participants maintain full responsibility for their personal decisions and daily activities.

2. Non-Service Environment
The program does not provide medical care, supervision, or custodial services.

3. Voluntary Participation
All individuals participate voluntarily and may leave at any time in accordance with program policies.

4. Personal Responsibility
Participants remain responsible for their finances, personal care, and adherence to community standards.

5. Community Respect
All participants must respect the safety, rights, and property of fellow residents.

6. Safety and Preservation
All participants share responsibility for maintaining a safe and respectful living environment.

Program Governance
This charter governs the operation of participating residences and establishes the foundational doctrine of the NILPF framework.

Doctrine Adherence
All participants, operators, and associated parties must operate within the principles and standards established in this charter and the accompanying program doctrine.
